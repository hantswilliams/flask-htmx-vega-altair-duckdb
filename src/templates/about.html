About the template...

The primary purpose of the Flask-Altair template project is to bridge the gap between web application development and interactive data visualization. At its core, the aim to simplify and expedite the process of integrating robust and responsive data visualizations into web applications. By leveraging the strengths of Flask, a lightweight yet powerful web framework, coupled with the capabilities of Altair, a declarative statistical visualization library, the project offers an efficient and modular approach to web-based data presentation.
